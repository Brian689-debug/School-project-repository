Requirements for registration.

To register to our school one is required to have a K.C.S.E certificate.One is also required to have paid full school fees so that one can be admitted to our institution.

Please note that school fees cannot be paid via cheque.You can also pay school fees through monthly installments but ensure you have paid in full by the end of the first term.

Thankyou for choosing our institution.
